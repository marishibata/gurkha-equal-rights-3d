Coming soon...a history of Gurkha injustices

Over 46,000 Nepalese Gurkha soldiers have died in the 200 years fighting as members of the British Army - yet they still don't have access to the same rights as British soldiers.

Our campaign for equal military pension rates has been reported by The Guardian, BBC News and others, with the UK Government agreeing to talks following coverage.
